District 9

Panem's bread bowl, providing the fertile harvest we need to keep rising as a nation.

↓

The industry is centered around farming, though the people face difficult working conditions, as in most of the other districts.

The Stalk Rows

Endless rows of tall grain stalks that appear to stretch on forever. At night, the rows create eerie shadows, and the wind moving through them sounds like whispers, often making the workers feel as though they are being watched.

Grainspire

A towering grain silo at the district’s center, where vast quantities of harvested grain are stored and processed. It looms over the district like an unyielding fortress.

The Winnow Docks

Where grain shipments are loaded onto large, mechanized barges or railcars, headed to the Capitol. This area is always active, with oversized machines and cranes transferring bags and crates, creating a constant buzz of motion.

Thresher Fields

Expansive golden farmlands with massive mechanical threshers operating throughout the day during harvest season. A symbol of abundance, yet plagued by harsh labor quotas.
