District 4

We love fishing and anything related to water

↓

Making fishhooks, using nets, tridents, swimming and identifying sea life... The oceanic culture defines us.

Merrow's End

Quiet, sacred coastal grove of tidepools where locals leave offerings to sea spirits for luck before the Hunger Games Reaping. It's not marked on Capitol maps, but every local knows where it is.

The Siren's Helm

Elite military and training facility, perched on a rugged cliffside overlooking the churning sea. Built partially into the rock and extending into the ocean itself, it resembles a colossal sea fortress.

Coralspire lighthouse

The "Capitol's Eye Over the Bay". It's an old, towering lighthouse that was converted into a Capitol outpost, it casts a violet light over the bay.

Coral Docks

A bustling harbor surrounded by fishing fleets, seafood markets, and net-weaving stations. Serves as both economic and social heart of the district.
