District 5

A mix of industrial starkness, cutting-edge tech, and eerie beauty shaped by its role in fueling the Capitol.

↓

Power and enegy production supplying the entire nation of Panem, including the Capitol's bright lights.

The Atlas Dam

Named after the titan who held up the sky and with massive concrete supports, overflow spillways, and dozens of rotating turbines roaring below the surface, it's the largest and most sacred piece of infrastructure.

Lumen Steps

More than a way to get from one level to another, they are a living art piece, a functional energy source with kinetic tiles that light up as people walk symbolizing the motto: “Energy from the People".

Circuit Row

Stretching across the eastern edge of the district like an electrical artery, buzzing gently with purpose and potential stands a long strip of interconnected housing blocks, glowing with soft light from rooftop solar panels and energy meters.
