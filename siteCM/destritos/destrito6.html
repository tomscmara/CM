District 6

Transportation, automobile production and... drugs??

↓

Filled with tracks, terminals, fumes and forgotten tech it's a very industrial landscape.

The Train Yard

Central hub for transportation logistics - loading, unloading, repairs, and sorting of trains and freight.

Echo Crossing

Old transport bridge used as a vigil space. Covered in candles, scrap tokens, and names of lost drivers. Locals claim to hear ghost trains on windless nights.

The Rush Rooms

Illicit dens with black-lit interiors with glowing murals made of fluorescent chalk and broken LED scraps where "Morphling" is the most used drug to escape escape the harsh grind of reality.
