The Nut

Carved into the side of a mountain, a massive mountain stronghold serving as the Capitol’s primary military base and weapons storage facility.

The Colossus Quarry

An expansive dangerous open-pit excavation site where workers mine massive slabs of marble, granite, and other valuable stone for the Capitol’s grand architecture and where accidents are frequent and survival depends on skill and endurance.

The Ashen Path

A long road lined with statues of past victors and soldiers, leading to the district’s central government buildings.

The Citadel

A fortified compound housing the Peacekeeper training academy, weapons manufacturing facilities, and barracks. It stands on a plateau, symbolizing strength and vigilance.
