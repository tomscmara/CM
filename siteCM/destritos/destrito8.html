District 8

All about textile production and industrial grind.

↓

With clothing factories, loom halls, and thick clouds of pollution we were still the first to rebel after the 74th games.

The Bannerworks

A restricted Capitol facility where propaganda textiles are created—flags, armbands, and stitched emblems of the Capitol and Snow. No one speaks while working here.

Fabrication Wall

It's an interactive digital billboard from the Capitol, displaying quotas and model worker stats. Locals call it the “Wall of Lies.”

The Stitchspire

A towering Capitol-run textile plant at the district’s center that has gleaming chrome needles crown in the roof, constantly emitting steam.
