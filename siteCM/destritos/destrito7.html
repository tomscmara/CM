Ashfall Clearing

A wide forest gap where an old rebellion fire once raged. Charred stumps still remain, and Capitol sensors still buzz faintly nearby. No birds sing here. No animals tread. The air holds a permanent smell of smoke and memory.

The Timberline Hall

Central command for lumber operations. It's a grand wooden lodge reinforced with steel, filled with wall-sized maps of logging zones and Capitol quotas.
